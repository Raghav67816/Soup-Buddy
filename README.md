# Soup-Buddy

The world is ending! Do you want to spend your last hours in sadness, despair, or anxiety? NO!That is why we are introducing Soup Buddy! Many people have been saying soup is evil 😭, but that is just anti-soup propaganda. He is here to make you happy! 🥹Soup Buddy simply attaches to your laptop. If he sees that you are sad, he will give you a big hug (or a slap) to make you happy again! Why on a laptop, you ask? Well, we know you will still want to be grinding through all those YSWS programs before you die, and we don't want sadness getting in your way!

![alt-text](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Raghav67816/Soup-Buddy/refs/heads/main/banner.jpeg)

## Features
 - Modular design
 - Fits on screens from 14 inch - 15.6 inch displays (laptops and monitors both)
 - Comes with cute animations (starting with default blinking)
 - Boinks you when you are sad, depends on his mood

## How Does It Work ?

ESP32 starts an AP and gives users option to select the WiFi to connect to. ESP32 then connects to a MQTT server.
